My Interests

My interests are varied and numerous. But the common ground is they are all useful to people in general.
Afterall everything is about the people.

Futurism

Reading about, imagining and making some contribution to a better future for people.
A lot of interesting things will be happening -
  • Electric Vehicles
  • Vehicles running on fuel cells
  • Vertical Farming
  • Power generation
  • Healthcare
  • 3D Printing

These are just some of the fields where a lot of changes and improvements can be made.
For example - 3D printing materials are still expensive and hence have found little application in niche areas although the potential is huge.
Cleaner transportation requires more infrastructure for rechargin/refuelling, maintainence etc.
Agriculture is also responsible for a lot of emissions like fossil fuels and require a revolution.
Healthcare is becoming more and more expensive in terms of time and money both. It should not become a luxury and hence needs an overhaul(or several of them).

There are already lots of innovations happening in these areas but many do not scale to have a major effect. That too is something we need to work on.
